Context

Prolians (part of Descours & Cabaud group) is launching a B2B e-commerce site and wants to convert offline customers to buy online too. The initial analysis shows that 5% of omnichannel customers generate 40% of revenue, while offline sales are 50x bigger than online.

The goal: find which offline customers to target by email to maximize online conversion and grow digital revenue.

Mission

Analyze 4 customer datasets to identify a target group for email campaigns and maximize offline → omnichannel conversion, with the end goal of growing prolians.fr revenue.

Volume: 1M+ transactions · 77K+ product lines Scope: Oct 2024 - Oct 2025 · 5 French regions Type: Buying behavior & website navigation · Product catalog

Impact

€30M potential web revenue over 1 year If we fully convert the identified target group (assuming 30% of omnichannel customer revenue goes online)
500 target customers identified Craft sector · Active online account · €100M revenue (out of €3B total offline) · Optimized similarity score
Conversion strategy activated Email campaign currently being deployed · Scoring method validated and ready for reuse
Deliverables: Executive presentation · Similarity score for 18,000 offline customers · Prioritized list of 500 target customers · Email content recommendations

Approach

Data-driven method in 6 steps

  • Channel analysis: Revenue mapping by channel → Found omnichannel as the star channel (40% revenue with only 5% of customers)
  • RFM segmentation (K-means): Clustering based on Recency, Frequency, Monetary value → High-value customers are concentrated in omnichannel (+15% vs online only)
  • Strategic decision: Choose between growing online organically vs converting offline → Focus on offline→omnichannel conversion (50x more offline revenue = maximum impact)
  • Omnichannel profiling: Created ideal customer profile using buying behavior and website navigation data
  • Cosine similarity scoring: Calculated score for 18,000 offline customers vs ideal omnichannel profile → Found offline customers most likely to behave like omnichannel ones
  • Actionable filtering: Added practical filters (active online account + craft sector) + sorted by revenue → Final list of 500 prioritized customers

Feedback

🏆 Award won at the final presentation – Prolians marketing and data teams validated the approach and analysis quality. The campaign is currently being deployed on the identified target group.

Personal reflection: With more behavioral data (web tracking, email interactions, 2-3 years history), we could have built a predictive model for conversion probability, allowing better ROI by targeting high-potential customers while reducing the decision cycle. However, the cosine similarity scoring was a solid first approach that could be activated quickly.

Leadership & mentoring: I worked with first-year students who were less comfortable with technical work. This project was an opportunity to create a clear plan from the start, explain each analytical step, and get 100% productivity from the team. A real mentoring exercise that I really enjoyed.
